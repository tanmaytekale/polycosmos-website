dream cinema uses the reincarnation and simulation tech to connect the ai avatars with personality-embedded agentic behaviour. the project is at its initial stage.

280 is the average crew size of a movie; two hundred and eighty different perspectives involved to create one project. every single perspective is different, bringing in their own strength, weakness and uniqueness to the output. these diverse impressions working together on a pipleline makes it complicated, however this complexity shapes the result that we watch on the screen. dream cinema is an endevour to simulate these complex processes to get a realistic and cinematic outcome.
